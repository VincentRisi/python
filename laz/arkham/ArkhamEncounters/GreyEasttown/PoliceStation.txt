direction. Pass a Luck (-1) check to avoid
getting shot. If you fail, lose 2 Stamina.

holding his gun. You may search the

card and take it.

PSTATION

Dingby to share some files with you that

PSTATION

step into his office to discuss the recent
strange events. Pass a Luck (-2) check to
convince him to take you into his confidence

PSTATION
One of the men in the holding cells tries to
intimidate you with stories about the things
that he,s seen. Make a Will (-1) check. If
you pass, gain 1 Clue token. If you fail,
move to the street and lose 1 Sanity as the
man laughs at your retreating back.

PSTATION

file as he makes his way past you. Pass a
